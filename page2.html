About

INDIA’S first news magazine aimed at children

A unique monthly magazine for the Children that focuses only on children. The magazine will cover a range of topics from current affairs, to sports, news, views, stories, historical facts, interesting anecdotes, health tips, great achievers who have had humble beginnings, and also have a section with contribution from the children.

Through this magazine, the child-like curiosity of the children will be aroused to know more about the world around them. This magazine is a quest for knowledge and will provide guidance to hone the skills of the children. There will be interactive sections for quizzes, puzzles, games, art, and a do-it-yourself kit that will keep the young minds engaged.

Why The Children?

With a proliferation of gadgets and technological advancements, the children these days are so involved in these gadgets that there is always a fear of radiation and damage to the eyes. To wean the children away from gadgets, while at the same time providing them quality information that will probe and kindle their naturally inquisitive minds, thereby inculcating in them the habit of reading is the key aim of this magazine.

What The Children Have?

Through this magazine, we help children explore everyday life around themselves through the eyes of science. We try to provide a background of how things have evolved in this world. This will make the children probe, thereby helping them acquire and accumulate knowledge. Most of all, this book will help children explore the pleasure of reading which will help children create or form images and ideas in their own minds, thereby kindling their creativity and imagination. The format of the magazine will make the children naturally inquisitive which will help them form considered judgement after exploring all possibilities, as they grow up. Also, the exposure to the use of word-play will enrich their vocabulary.

Why Opt For The Children?

While the content of the magazine is age appropriate, it also challenges the children’s analytical, logical, and comprehensive skills, thereby building life-skills for the children that will stand them good stead in their future. The magazine also builds the general knowledge of the children.
